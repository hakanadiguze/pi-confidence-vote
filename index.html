❓ How does this work? ›

1. Host creates a session with ART + team names.
2. Copy the invite link and share it with all participants.
3. Participants open the link and start voting immediately — no sign-in needed.
4. Click any 1–5 button on a team or ART card to vote.
5. Results update live for everyone. Votes are anonymous.
